"Sir, we used LOSO (Leave-One-Subject-Out) cross-validation. We trained on 25 people and tested on the 26th—someone the AI had never seen. The 0.99 score isn't memorization; it's the result of our Triple-Stream design filtering out noise."
"Why the Color Signal? Isn't motion (Optical Flow) enough?"
"Motion is noisy. Blinks or head shifts confuse it. But Blood Flow doesn't lie. By tracking Cb/Cr vascular signals, we have a physiological signature that validates the micro-expression."
"Why TCN? Why not a Transformer or an LSTM?"
"LSTMs forget the start of a clip. Transformers are too heavy. TCNs with Dilated Convolutions are parallel (fast) and catch temporal patterns across the whole expression window."
